## What Practitioners Actually Do

B2B creator agencies fill a genuine gap: the operational capacity to source, onboard, brief, manage, and pay creators at a scale or speed that an in-house CPM cannot match alone. The decision to use one is usually pragmatic — a campaign timeline, a headcount constraint, a vertical where the agency has pre-existing creator relationships that would take months to build independently.

The structural risk in agency relationships that practitioners rarely anticipate is the knowledge transfer problem. An agency that manages your creator relationships exclusively owns the relationship history — the context, the rapport, the creator's awareness of your brand's evolving strategy. When the relationship ends, that knowledge does not transfer. The next CPM starts from zero. The practitioners who avoid this outcome are the ones who insist on direct creator access and internal documentation from the first brief.
