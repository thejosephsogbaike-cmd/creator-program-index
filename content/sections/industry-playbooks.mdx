## What Practitioners Actually Do

B2B creator program strategy is not vertical-agnostic. The ICP for a B2B SaaS tool is reachable on LinkedIn. The ICP for an industrial hardware supplier is not. The content that builds trust with a CISO is not the content that builds trust with a CFO. The creator who reaches HR leaders at Series A companies has built a different audience, on different platforms, with different content cadences, than the creator who reaches procurement teams at public companies.

The practitioners who have moved between verticals — or who have built programs across multiple B2B industries simultaneously — describe the recalibration as more significant than they expected. The principles transfer: ICP concentration over reach, trust over exposure, relationship over transaction. The execution, the platforms, the creator types, the content formats, and the measurement frameworks are often vertical-specific in ways that generic B2B creator program guidance does not capture.
