## What Practitioners Actually Do

Most B2B creator program frameworks are built for the US market and exported with minimal adaptation. The LinkedIn-first, English-language, professional content model works reasonably well in English-speaking markets and some Northern European contexts. It requires meaningful adaptation in Southern and Eastern Europe, and significant reinvention in the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Sub-Saharan Africa — markets that are growing faster, building earlier, and in some cases ahead of the curve on B2B creator adoption.

The African B2B creator market deserves specific attention. The concentration of startup activity in Lagos, Nairobi, Cape Town, and Accra has produced a cohort of B2B-native creators — founders, operators, investors, and practitioners who are building audiences on LinkedIn and YouTube that are highly concentrated by ICP. The practitioners who have activated these creators early have found engagement rates and audience quality that are difficult to replicate in saturated US and UK markets.
