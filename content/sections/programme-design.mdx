## What Practitioners Actually Do

Most creator program guidance begins at execution — how to source creators, how to write a brief, how to measure results. This section begins one step earlier: how to design the programme that all of that execution serves.

Programme design covers the decisions that cannot be undone easily once the programme is running. How many creators to start with. Whether the programme is always-on or campaign-based. How the creator roster maps to the buyer journey. What the first 90 days looks like before any creator goes live. These decisions are made once, and their effects compound across every operational choice that follows.
