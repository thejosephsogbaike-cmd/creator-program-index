## What Practitioners Actually Do

B2B creator programs are not influencer marketing with a smaller budget.

The differences run deeper than the numbers. A B2C influencer campaign optimises for reach — how many people see the content. A B2B creator program optimises for ICP concentration — what percentage of the creator's audience holds the job title that can actually buy the product.

A creator with 8,000 followers who reaches CISOs at mid-market security companies is more commercially valuable to a B2B security tool than one with 200,000 followers who reaches a general technology audience. This is the foundational principle that every other section of this Index builds on.
