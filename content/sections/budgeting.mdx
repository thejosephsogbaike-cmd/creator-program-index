## What Practitioners Actually Do

The first B2B creator program budget is almost always too small and structured around the wrong cost categories. Creator fees get the most line-item scrutiny. The operational costs — the tools, the time, the agency support, the legal review — either go unbudgeted or land in other teams' cost centres where they become invisible and eventually contentious.

The practitioners who build sustainable multi-year programs have almost universally done one thing: they have built a budget model that reflects what the programme actually costs to run well, not what they thought finance would approve. Starting with the honest number — and then building the business case around it — produces more durable funding than starting with a number that feels safe and growing into reality one budget cycle at a time.
