## What Practitioners Actually Do

B2B creator programs are not platform-neutral. The decision about where a creator publishes — LinkedIn, YouTube, a podcast, a newsletter, a community — determines which buyers the content reaches and at what stage of their decision journey. A creator who publishes long-form video on YouTube reaches buyers in active learning mode. A creator who publishes daily on LinkedIn reaches buyers in passive awareness mode. A creator who sends a weekly email newsletter reaches buyers who have made an active choice to receive their perspective.

These are not interchangeable. A B2B security tool trying to reach CISOs at the point of vendor evaluation has a different creator format requirement than one trying to build brand familiarity six months before a procurement cycle opens. The practitioners who run the most efficient programs have matched their creator formats to specific buyer journey stages — not picked the format where their budget can reach the most impressions.
